"Passionate about affecting real, positive and sustainable change by empowering our most vulnerable to better their lives."

OUR STORY

The urgent need for people to step up and show up for those strugling to so for themselves, coupled with the overwhelming generosity provided by our personal network is what sparked the concept of #FEED1000.

Our immediate priority is to intercept and mitigate the devastating effects of the COVID pandemic on the most vulnerable in our society, in the form of cooked meals, food parcels and clothing for the fast approaching winter.

Lets liberate others with our spirit of Ubuntu, let's come together and seed hope in the minds of our fellow South Africans.

#FEED1000 TEAM

VISION

We look to be a go to supplier of food parcels and food items to those in need.

MISSION

To use our access and network to provide sustainable feeding solutions to those in need.
